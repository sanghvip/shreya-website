My philosophy

We are all capable of
extraordinary lives

I believe emotions are not the enemy — they are the compass. We often fall prey to the huge emotional elephant that sometimes controls the rational rider inside all of us. My work is about helping you understand that elephant, befriend it, and ultimately guide it.

With a challenging start to my own life, I understand more than most the importance of mental and emotional wellbeing — especially in the growing years. That lived experience shapes every session I hold, every curriculum I design, and every workshop I facilitate.

My story

A decade of building bridges between hearts and healing

I am a psychologist, founder, and Global Shapers Community alumna who has spent over a decade at the intersection of mental health, education, and human potential. My work began with a simple question — why do so many people who need care never access it? That question became my mission.

I founded The Making of You — first as a counselling space, then as a broader platform for social and emotional learning — because I believe that emotional intelligence is not a luxury. It is the foundation on which everything else is built.

Workshops, training programmes, and ongoing support for employees and leadership teams on managing stress, building emotional resilience, improving communication, and creating psychologically safe workplaces. Because the health of an organisation begins with the health of its people.

How I work

Therapy that is as individual
as you are

I do not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. Every person who walks into a session brings a unique story, a unique nervous system, and a unique set of needs. My role is to follow your lead, offer evidence-based tools when helpful, and hold space for whatever needs to emerge.

My work is grounded in empathy, warmth, and the conviction that change is possible for everyone — not as a result of willpower, but as a natural consequence of genuine understanding.

01
Safety first, always
Before any technique, any framework, any goal — the space must feel safe. Nothing good happens in therapy without trust.
02
You set the pace
There is no rush. We move at whatever speed feels right for you, revisiting what needs revisiting and celebrating every small shift.
03
Evidence-based, human-centred
I draw from established therapeutic frameworks while always keeping you — not the framework — at the centre of the work.
04
Beyond the session
Insights from therapy are most powerful when they carry into daily life. We build tools and practices you can actually use between sessions.
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
Identifying and restructuring unhelpful thought patterns that drive emotional distress — practical, evidence-based, and highly effective for anxiety and depression.
Emotion-Focused Approaches
Working with emotions as primary data — understanding what they are communicating, rather than suppressing or bypassing them.
Mindfulness-Based Techniques
Building present-moment awareness and the capacity to observe thoughts and feelings without being overwhelmed by them.
Strength-Based Perspective
Every person already has within them what they need to heal and grow. Therapy is about finding and amplifying those existing capacities.
Social & Emotional Learning Frameworks
For schools and organisations — evidence-based SEL models that build emotional intelligence as a teachable, measurable skill.
